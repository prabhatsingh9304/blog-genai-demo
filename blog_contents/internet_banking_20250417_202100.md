**What is Internet Banking?**

**Understanding the Basics**

Internet banking, also known as online banking, is a service that allows customers to conduct financial transactions over the Internet. It provides the convenience of managing your bank accounts, transferring money, paying bills, and much more, right from the comfort of your home or office.

**The Evolution of Online Banking**

- **Early Beginnings:** Initially, Internet banking was limited to basic functions like checking account balances and viewing recent transactions.
- **Advanced Features:** Over time, banks have expanded their online offerings to include services such as fund transfers, loan applications, and investment management.

**Advantages of Internet Banking**

**Convenience and Accessibility**

- **24/7 Access:** Manage your finances anytime, anywhere.
- **Global Reach:** Conduct international transactions with ease.
- **Paperless Transactions:** Reduce the need for physical documents and paperwork.

**Cost-Effective Solutions**

- **Reduced Fees:** Many banks offer lower fees for online transactions compared to traditional banking.
- **Time Savings:** Save time by avoiding bank visits and long queues.

**Enhanced Security Measures**

- **Secure Login:** Multi-factor authentication to protect your account.
- **Fraud Detection:** Advanced monitoring systems to prevent unauthorized access.

**Key Features of Internet Banking**

**Account Management**

- **Real-Time Updates:** Monitor account balances and transactions in real-time.
- **E-Statements:** Access monthly statements electronically.

**Fund Transfers**

- **Domestic Transfers:** Easily move money between accounts within the same bank or to other banks.
- **International Transfers:** Send money overseas with competitive exchange rates.
